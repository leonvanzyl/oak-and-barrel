# Oak & Barrel Design System

## Brand Overview

Oak & Barrel is a warm, inviting restaurant experience that combines rustic elegance with modern sophistication. The design language evokes the comfort of artisanal cuisine, aged spirits, and thoughtful hospitality.

### Design Philosophy

**"Warmth Through Craft"**

Every design decision should feel intentional, like a dish prepared by a skilled chef. We embrace:
- **Organic warmth** over sterile minimalism
- **Generous hospitality** in whitespace and layout
- **Artisanal character** in typography and details
- **Appetizing presentation** in imagery and color

## Typography

### Font Stack

**Display Font: Playfair Display**
- Usage: Hero headlines, section titles
- Weights: 400, 500, 700

**Script Font: Dancing Script**
- Usage: Taglines, special callouts
- Weight: 500, 700

**Body Font: Source Sans 3**
- Usage: Body text, UI elements, navigation
- Weights: 400, 500, 600, 700
